Sample Context: This change communication email follows OCM best practices using the ADKAR framework. Each element builds user readiness for the technology transition.
A = Awareness D = Desire K = Knowledge Ab = Ability R = Reinforcement
Subject: Action Required: New Expense Reporting System Launches March 15

Dear UCF Faculty and Staff,

What is Changing Awareness

Beginning March 15, UCF will transition from the legacy expense system to Workday Expenses. This upgrade is part of our ongoing commitment to streamline administrative processes and improve your experience.

Why This Matters to You Desire

  • Faster reimbursements: Average processing time reduced from 14 days to 5 days
  • Mobile access: Submit expenses and upload receipts from your phone
  • Simplified approvals: Single-click approval for managers
  • Real-time tracking: See exactly where your expense report is in the approval process

What You Need to Do Knowledge Ability

  1. Complete required training (15 minutes) by March 10: [Training Link]
  2. Submit any pending expenses in the current system by March 12
  3. Download the Workday app to your mobile device (optional but recommended)

Resources and Support Reinforcement

  • Quick-Start Guide: [Link to guide]
  • Video Tutorial: [Link to 3-minute overview video]
  • FAQ: [Link to knowledge base]
  • Live Office Hours: March 13-14, 10am-2pm, Tech Commons (drop-in help)

Questions? Reply to this email or submit a ticket through ServiceNow.

Thank you for your partnership in making this transition smooth.

UCF IT Change Management Team
