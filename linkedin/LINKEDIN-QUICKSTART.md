## The "Why" Behind These Changes

| Change | Why It Matters |
|--------|----------------|
| Value-focused headline | Recruiters scan headlines in 2 seconds. Job titles blend in; value statements stand out. |
| Narrative About section | Stories are memorable. Bullet points are forgettable. |
| Featured portfolio links | Puts your best work above the fold. UTM tracking shows who's clicking. |
| Achievement-first bullets | "$200K grant" catches eyes faster than "developed curriculum" |
| Pinned skills | Controls what endorsements you get; signals expertise |

## LinkedIn Algorithm Tips

1. **Complete = Visible**: LinkedIn shows complete profiles more often in search
2. **Headline keywords matter**: Recruiters search by title/skill - include "Instructional Design" and "Change Management"
3. **Featured section boosts engagement**: Profiles with Featured content get more views
4. **Activity matters**: Post or comment weekly to stay visible
