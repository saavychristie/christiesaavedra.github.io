The Opportunity

The beauty and wellness industry was growing rapidly, but Arizona lacked accessible, high-quality esthetics training programs. Many aspiring professionals had to travel out of state or settle for subpar education.

I saw an opportunity to create a vocational training program that would meet state licensing requirements while providing practical, career-ready skills.

Building From Scratch

As co-founder, I was responsible for the instructional design and curriculum development aspects of launching the school:

  • Researched Arizona State Board of Cosmetology requirements
  • Designed comprehensive curriculum covering all licensing competencies
  • Created student handbooks, course materials, and assessment tools
  • Developed hands-on training protocols and safety procedures

Partnership Development

Sustainable enrollment required building relationships with local school districts and community organizations:

  • Presented program benefits to school district career counselors
  • Established referral partnerships with high schools
  • Created marketing materials targeting career changers
  • Built relationships with local salons for job placement

Results & Impact

The Arizona Esthetics Institute successfully launched and received state approval:

  • Full state approval from Arizona State Board of Cosmetology
  • Complete curriculum covering 600+ hours of required training
  • Partnerships with multiple school districts
  • Graduates successfully passing state licensing exams
