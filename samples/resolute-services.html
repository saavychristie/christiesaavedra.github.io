The Challenge

A Medicaid-based mental health agency was drowning in manual processes. Billing took 40-80 hours per week, intake processing consumed entire days, and staff spent more time on paperwork than patient care. Compliance requirements added additional complexity.

The agency needed a complete operational overhaul—not just new software, but a fundamental rethinking of how work gets done.

The Solution

I co-founded Resolute Services to address these challenges systematically. Our approach combined:

  • Business process analysis to identify inefficiencies and automation opportunities
  • Custom software development tailored to Medicaid compliance requirements
  • Change management to ensure staff adoption and sustained improvement
  • Training programs to build internal capability

Implementation

The transformation happened in phases, allowing staff to adapt while maintaining operations:

  • Phase 1: Mapped existing workflows and identified pain points
  • Phase 2: Designed and built custom billing automation system
  • Phase 3: Integrated payroll processing with service documentation
  • Phase 4: Trained all staff and established support processes

Results & Impact

The transformation delivered measurable results across the organization:

  • Billing processing reduced from 40-80 hours/week to 15 minutes
  • Intake processing cut from 10 hours to 15 minutes per patient
  • 90% of manual processes automated
  • Staff freed to focus on patient care instead of paperwork
