The Challenge

Palmview K-8 had over 500 devices across computer labs, classrooms, and administrative offices. Teachers and staff frequently encountered technical issues that disrupted instruction, and the lack of documentation meant every problem required direct IT support.

The school needed a sustainable approach to technology support that would empower users to solve common problems independently.

Building the Knowledge Base

I applied Stack Overflow's proven question-and-answer methodology to create a searchable knowledge base:

  • Documented common issues with step-by-step solutions
  • Created visual guides with screenshots for complex procedures
  • Organized content by device type, software, and problem category
  • Built quick-reference cards for the most frequent issues

Training & Adoption

Documentation is only useful if people can find and use it. I focused on making the knowledge base accessible:

  • Trained teachers during professional development days
  • Created laminated quick-reference cards for each computer lab
  • Established a simple process for reporting new issues
  • Updated documentation based on feedback and new problems

Results & Impact

The knowledge base transformed how the school handled technology support:

  • Teachers could resolve common issues without waiting for IT support
  • Reduced interruptions to instruction time
  • New staff could onboard faster with comprehensive documentation
  • Created a sustainable system that outlasted any individual
