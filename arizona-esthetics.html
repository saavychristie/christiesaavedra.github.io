The Challenge

Arizona lacked accessible vocational training options for esthetics. At-risk youth in particular had few pathways to licensed, employable careers in the beauty industry.

I saw an opportunity to create something from nothing.

  • No existing program to model‚Äîhad to build from scratch
  • Required navigating Arizona State Board regulatory requirements
  • Needed to develop state-approved curriculum meeting licensing exam standards
  • Had to build facility, recruit students, and establish partnerships simultaneously
