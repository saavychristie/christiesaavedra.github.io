The Challenge

Utah's tech industry faced a critical developer shortage. Xactware, a leading property data and technology company, needed qualified Junior Developers but couldn't find enough local talent. Traditional educational programs weren't keeping pace with rapidly evolving industry needs.

  • Curriculum outdated within months of publication
  • No direct pipeline from education to employment
  • Students lacked real-world project experience

Lasting Impact

The UCAP program evolved into Utah's "Talent Ready Utah" initiative, which has since distributed over $2.1 million in grants and created capacity for over 5,800 individuals to earn industry-recognized credentials. The Xactware-MTECH partnership model demonstrated that industry-education collaboration could solve talent pipeline challenges at scale.
